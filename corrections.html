Corrections

How ContextWire reviews corrections and updates.

Readers can request article corrections, source additions, image credit changes, and clarity updates.

How to Request a Correction

Email sattarwajithboop@gmail.com with the article URL, the sentence or image that needs review, and a public source that supports the correction.

What We Review

ContextWire reviews factual errors, outdated details, missing context, broken source links, image-credit concerns, and wording that could make a developing topic sound more certain than the sources support.

How Updates Are Handled

When a correction is accepted, the article may be updated, clarified, expanded with a better source, or removed if the page cannot meet the site's editorial standard. Updated dates are shown on article pages.

Response Expectations

Correction requests are reviewed as soon as practical. Urgent requests involving safety, legal risk, health, finance, or personal reputation should include clear evidence and the exact page URL.
