Terms, Editorial Notes, and Image Credits

These terms explain how TrendPulse Daily handles article information, image use, corrections, and reader responsibility.

Last updated: May 16, 2026

Editorial Purpose

TrendPulse Daily publishes reader-friendly explainers about topics receiving public attention. Articles are intended for general information, quick orientation, and safer follow-up research. They should not be treated as legal, financial, medical, or professional advice.

Editorial Review

Published pages should be reviewed for usefulness, clarity, image credits, and unsupported claims. Articles are designed to help readers understand the topic, identify what is known, and know what details still need checking.

Accuracy and Fast-Moving Topics

Search trends and news topics can change quickly. Articles may describe what readers are searching for, why attention may be increasing, and what details need verification. Readers should confirm important facts through official sources, trusted reporting, direct statements, or primary documents before making decisions.

Image Policy

Article images are selected to represent the topic category or context. They may be original editorial illustrations or sourced from public/free stock providers such as Unsplash, with the provider credited near the article image.

We avoid using copyrighted celebrity, athlete, broadcaster, company, or news-agency photos unless a suitable license or permission is available. Some images are illustrative rather than documentary. For example, a sports article may use a stadium or court image instead of a photo of a named player.

Corrections and Removal Requests

If a page appears inaccurate, outdated, confusing, or uses an image incorrectly, contact us with the article URL and the detail that needs review. TrendPulse Daily may update, clarify, replace, or remove content when a reasonable request is verified.

External Links

Articles may refer readers to official pages, public records, trusted publishers, ticket platforms, or other external resources. We are not responsible for the content, privacy practices, pricing, or availability of third-party websites.

Credits

Image credits are displayed near article images. Original editorial illustrations and stock images are credited where available.
