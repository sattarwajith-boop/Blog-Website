Image Use, Credits, and Permissions

TrendPulse Daily uses public, free-stock, AI-generated, or permission-based editorial images to support source-linked automated articles.

Image Policy

Article images are selected to represent the topic category or context. They may be AI-generated by TrendPulse Daily or sourced from public/free stock providers such as Unsplash, with the provider credited on the article image.

We avoid using copyrighted celebrity, athlete, broadcaster, company, or news-agency photos unless a suitable license or permission is available. If a future article uses a permission-based image, that permission should be recorded before publication.

Some images are illustrative rather than documentary. For example, a tennis article may use a tennis court image instead of a photo of the named player, and an entertainment article may use a cinema or stage image instead of a specific performer.

Fair Use and Removal

Fair use depends on context and is not guaranteed. If a rightsholder believes an image is used incorrectly, they can request review, replacement, or removal. TrendPulse Daily should prefer licensed, public, or permissioned material whenever possible.

Editorial Content

Automated articles summarize trend signals and linked source headlines. Readers should verify fast-moving details from the linked sources before relying on them.

Credits

Image credits are displayed near article images. AI-generated images are marked as such. Stock images are credited to their provider.
