Privacy Policy

This policy explains how TrendPulse Daily handles visitor data, contact emails, cookies, analytics, advertising, and third-party services.

Last updated: May 16, 2026

Information We Collect

TrendPulse Daily does not require visitors to create accounts, log in, or submit personal information to read articles. If you contact us by email, we receive the information you choose to send, such as your email address, message, article URL, and any correction details you provide.

Technical Data

Like most websites, our hosting provider and third-party services may process basic technical information such as IP address, browser type, device information, referring page, pages visited, and approximate time of access. This information is generally used for security, performance, abuse prevention, and site improvement.

Cookies and Advertising

If advertising services such as Google AdSense are added, advertising partners may use cookies, device identifiers, or similar technologies to measure ad performance, prevent fraud, limit repeated ads, and show relevant advertising. Visitors can manage cookies through browser settings and, where available, through advertising preference tools provided by the advertising partner.

Analytics

If analytics tools are used, they may collect aggregated information about page views, device types, traffic sources, and reading behavior. We use this information to understand which articles are useful and to improve navigation and content quality.

External Services

The site may load fonts, images, hosting assets, embedded links, or advertising scripts from third-party providers. These providers may process technical data according to their own privacy policies. Examples may include GitHub Pages hosting, Google Fonts, image providers, analytics providers, and advertising partners.

How We Use Contact Messages

Messages sent to our contact email may be used to answer questions, review correction requests, investigate image-credit concerns, and improve articles. We do not sell contact messages or use them to create reader accounts.

Children's Privacy

TrendPulse Daily is a general information site and is not designed to collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has sent personal information, contact us and we will review the request.
